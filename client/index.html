Have you heard about Accell!?

It is an open-source command-line tool to quickly generating boilerplates of different stacks.

Try it outGet in touch

Be Productive

Previously, you have to do everything on your own. Bootstrap all the basic template. With Accell, we have changed your focus to be more productive by elimating all the grunt work.

Be Efficient

Accell is simple and easy-to-use. You don't need to create all the directories, files, do configurations. Accell is efficiently designed to take care of everything for you.

Save Time

Earlier, you spend hours configuring your project, setting up frontend, writing backend starter code, integrating database, etc. With Accell, all can be done under two minutes.

Open-source

Accell is entirely open-source and licensed under MIT. It means anyone is free to use the code and build something with it. And on top, it provides an opportunity for developers worldwide to contribute to it.

Support

Accell supports a wide variety of technologies for both frontend and backend. It also currently supports three databases. So you can generate web stacks by just picking up technologies you like in it.

Maintained

Accell is backed by a team of four developers who ensures you are getting what you are expecting. They are constantly introducing new features, fixing bugs, making releases, and more.
